This tool uses publicly shared data to estimate airdrop value. Here's how the calculation works:

  • Total Points:
    2024: 342,000,000,000
    2025: 543,000,000,000
  • Estimated Wallets:
    2024: 334,000
    2025: 291,000
  • Average Points per Wallet:
    2024: ~1,023,952
    2025: ~1,865,979
  • Airdrop Allocation:
    8% for 2024 and 5% for 2025 of total token supply

The numbers 69,674,265 and 34,675,097 are manually entered sample values used for estimation and may differ from user-specific allocations.
